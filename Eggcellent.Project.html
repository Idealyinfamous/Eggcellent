I love eggs!

Eggs are not only delicious but they are lil magic makers when it comes to cooking. How you ask?

Eggs contain multiple proteins that gel at different temperatures within the yolk and the white, and the temperature determines the gelling time. So, at one temperature you can have a fried egg and and at another temperature you can have a soft boiled yolky deliciousness.
